What Have I Been Up To?

Well, for starters, I have been going back to church and reconnecting with my faith. I have a scheduled appointment with my Bishop today to make plans to visit one of the nearby temples. I've had a temple recommend for a while, and even though I've been a lifelong member of the LDS church, I haven't actually been to a temple! So, I am in the process of "fixing" this.

Other than that, I had a terrific Thanksgiving. My aunts and cousins came to visit, and they brought a ton of sodas, including a seasonal Canada Dry fruit-flavored ginger ale! Oh, and speaking of sodas - I've recently discovered butterscotch beer from Costco! Boy, oh boy, is this stuff delicious; finally, a drink that tastes just like butter beer from Harry Potter!

I've also been making a little bit of money! My mom and I have been DoorDashing for the past month or so. It's a great way to make a little bit of cash. Generally, we only accept orders that pay at least a dollar per mile in tips. Sometimes, we wind up going way out in the woods. It's funny, I've lived in this town my whole life, but I am still learning about the existence of places that exist just a few miles outside of city limits. Typically, Panda Express is the busiest restaurant in town by a country mile; last time, it took us an hour for them to finish our order! Not worth it!

Lastly, we finally have electricity in our home again! The owner of the house came up and jerry-rigged the breaker for us; let's see how long it lasts! Also, my mom cursed out the landlord, and we formally got an eviction notice. Hooray! Now, we just have to find a place to live in the next three months. But first, we will be taking the rental agency and the landlord to court for making us live without electricity for literal months, which is not cool and not legal!
